Frequently asked questions

1. What if the employer reports that the candidate already exists?

Candidates are identified as duplicates only when the candidates apply for or agree to be referred to the employer’s hiring position before Recruitery sends candidate profiles to the Client and in the preceding 12 months. When reporting candidate duplicates, the employer needs to provide the source and the time of having candidates in their database to Recruitery.
